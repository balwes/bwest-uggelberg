The data displayed in this graph follows the SIR model. SIR categorizes humans as either (1) Suspectible, those who can be infected, (2) Infected, those who are infected and can infect others, and (3) Removed, those who have had the disease. Note that "removed" also includes deceased individuals.

Limitations

This website was made for a course in computer science and should not be substituted for information from health organizations. Please view this page as an introduction to SIR modeling using COVID-19 data. Also keep in mind that models are a simplification of reality. Search the web if you want to learn more about SIR and models.
